Quality Control

Peer review designed for fairness and improvement

A clear peer review page strengthens trust among authors, reviewers, and readers.

Peer Review Policy

Tanglaw uses peer review to support fairness, quality, and constructive improvement of submissions. The editorial office may adapt the review workflow according to article type, reviewer availability, and the maturity of the journal.

Typical review flow

  1. Initial editorial screening
  2. Assignment to reviewers when appropriate
  3. Reviewer comments and editorial decision
  4. Author revision
  5. Final acceptance or rejection

Default model

The journal may use double-blind peer review by default whenever practical, especially for formal research manuscripts. The editorial office may also use editorial review for announcements, notices, or selected special content.

Reviewer expectations

Reviewers should provide respectful, evidence-based, and timely feedback focused on quality, clarity, coherence, and appropriateness for publication.
