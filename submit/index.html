Submission

Email-based manuscript submission

Submission route

Tanglaw uses email submission only. Authors should send their manuscript and any supporting files to samueljohn.parreno@deped.gov.ph. A guided email composer is included below.

Compose a submission email

Submission checklist

  • Complete manuscript file
  • Abstract and keywords included
  • Correct author names and affiliations
  • References checked for consistency
  • Tables, figures, and appendices attached if needed
  • Required declarations prepared where applicable
