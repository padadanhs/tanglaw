About the journal

Tanglaw is designed as a formal yet welcoming publication space for emerging and practicing school-based researchers. It is hosted by Padada National High School but intentionally framed as a broader Philippine venue where junior high school students, senior high school students, and teachers can publish quality research, action research, investigatory projects, capstone outputs, and innovation studies.

Sections of the journal

Humanities, Arts, and Education

Research on learning, culture, language, pedagogy, and creative inquiry.

Science, Technology, Engineering, and Mathematics

Experimental, computational, and analytical work across school STEM domains.

Social Sciences and Business

Studies in society, community, economics, entrepreneurship, and applied social research.

Innovation, Capstone, and Product Development

Project-based outputs, prototypes, design studies, and applied product development.

Action Research

Classroom, school, and program-level improvement studies led by teachers and practitioners.

Student Investigatory Projects

Research and development outputs from guided student inquiry and experimentation.
