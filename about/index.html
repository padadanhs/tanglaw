About the Journal

A formal publication space for school-based research

Hosted by Padada National High School and open to junior high, senior high, and teacher submissions from across the Philippines.

Our identity

Tanglaw is a formal online journal created to support a culture of research in Philippine basic education. While it is a journal initiative of Padada National High School, it is intentionally designed to welcome submissions from schools across the country. Its identity combines academic seriousness, a youth-facing spirit, and a national orientation that avoids institutional exclusivity.

The name Tanglaw evokes illumination, guidance, and the emergence of ideas into public view. This makes it suitable for a journal that highlights the work of young scholars, student innovators, and teacher-researchers.

Who may publish

  • Junior high school students
  • Senior high school students
  • Teacher-researchers and school practitioners
  • Student-teacher collaborative teams

How the journal is positioned
