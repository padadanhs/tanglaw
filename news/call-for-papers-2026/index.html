Call for Papers for the Inaugural 2026 Volume

March 19, 2026

Tanglaw invites original submissions in Humanities, Arts, and Education; STEM; Social Sciences and Business; Innovation and Product Development; Action Research; and Student Investigatory Projects. As a rolling-publication journal, accepted papers will be published online once editorial and peer review processes are completed.
