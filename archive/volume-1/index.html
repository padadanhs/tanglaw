Topological Characterization and Phylogenetic Analysis of SARS-CoV-2 Genomic RNA Using Chaos Geometry and Persistent Homology

Research ArticleScience and TechnologyMarch 24, 2026

Jun Nathaniel G. Ratilla, Perie T. Miego, Nilmae Love P. Ngujo

This study applies chaos geometry and persistent homology to SARS-CoV-2 genomic RNA and shows that topology-derived features can reveal structural relationships among major variants of concern.

Discovery of Genetic Subgroups in Dyslexia Using Unsupervised Learning

Research ArticleScience and TechnologyMarch 24, 2026

Prince Niño V. Baltolore, Perie T. Miego, Maeru L. Sardido

Using openly shared genotype data, this study applies a cases-only unsupervised learning workflow and identifies several latent genetic subgroups within individuals who self-report dyslexia.
