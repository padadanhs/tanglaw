Privacy

Responsible handling of editorial and author information

This page helps clarify how the journal manages personal and submission-related data.

Privacy Statement

Tanglaw respects the privacy of authors, editors, reviewers, and readers. Personal information submitted through journal communication channels will be used only for publication-related purposes, editorial correspondence, and journal administration.

What may be collected

  • Names and affiliations
  • Email addresses
  • Submission-related metadata
  • Editorial correspondence records

How information is used

  • Communication about submissions
  • Editorial processing
  • Peer review coordination
  • Publication record keeping

Public visibility

Published articles may display author names, affiliations, abstracts, and other normal publication metadata. Reviewer identities should remain confidential unless the journal formally adopts and declares an open-review model.
