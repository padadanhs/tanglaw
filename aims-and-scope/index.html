Editorial Scope

Research, action, innovation, and inquiry in Philippine schools

The journal accommodates both student-led and teacher-led scholarship across six major thematic sections.

Aims and Scope

Tanglaw publishes original and meaningful school-based research and scholarly outputs that contribute to knowledge, classroom improvement, innovation, and community understanding. The journal seeks to showcase the intellectual work of junior high school students, senior high school students, and teachers in accessible but academically serious form.

It welcomes empirical, theoretical, descriptive, developmental, and reflective contributions appropriate to school contexts and emerging researchers.
